About Semtech & My Mission

Semtech Corporation

Semtech Corporation is a leading global supplier of high-performance analog and mixed-signal semiconductors and advanced algorithms. The company provides critical infrastructure solutions for IoT, data centers, and telecommunications worldwide.

With pioneering LoRa® devices and wireless RF technology, Semtech enables smart IoT applications that solve critical challenges facing our planet. The company's solutions power the backbone of modern connectivity infrastructure.

Operating globally with a commitment to innovation, Semtech transforms industries through smart connectivity solutions that improve lives and enable digital transformation.

GNOC Team & My Mission

Global Network Operations Center (GNOC) ensures 24/7 network reliability, monitoring critical infrastructure and maintaining optimal performance with minimal downtime across global operations.

My Role: As a GNOC intern reporting to Asim Khamari, I'm leveraging cutting-edge AI/ML technologies to automate manual processes, enhance incident detection accuracy, and revolutionize network monitoring capabilities.

Key Impact: Developing intelligent automation solutions that reduce incident response time by up to 70%, improve detection accuracy, and streamline GNOC team workflows through AI-powered tools.
